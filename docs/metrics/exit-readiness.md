# Exit Readiness Metrics

Critical metrics for assessing exit readiness:

## Product-Market Fit
- Market Share
- Customer Satisfaction
- Category Leadership
- Growth Trajectory

## Technical Foundation
- Architecture Scalability
- Code Quality
- Security Posture
- Technical Debt Level

## Growth Potential
- TAM Penetration
- Geographic Expansion
- Product Extension
- Platform Potential

## Risk Assessment
- Customer Concentration
- Technology Stack
- Team Dependencies
- Market Position
